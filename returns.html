Returns Policy

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return any item for a full refund. The return period starts from the day you receive your order.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be in original, unused condition
  • All original packaging and tags must be included
  • Items must not show signs of wear or damage
  • Electronics must be in working condition

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@Blyynx.com or call 1-800-1234567 to initiate a return
  2. You'll receive a Return Authorization (RA) number and return shipping label
  3. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  4. Attach the return label and ship the package back to us
  5. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Process

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Processing times may vary by payment provider:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Items that have been used or damaged
  • Items without original packaging
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Digital products or software
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago
