Shipping Information

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. This applies to all destinations within the United States.

Standard Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days from the date your order is processed. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50 | Free for orders over $50

Express Shipping

Need your items faster? Choose express shipping at checkout for delivery within 2-3 business days.

Cost: $14.99 (available for all orders)

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international destinations. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Please contact us for more information about shipping to your country.

Note: International orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package's journey to your doorstep. Visit our Track Your Order page to check your shipment status.
